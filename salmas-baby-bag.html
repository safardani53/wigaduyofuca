Salma's baby bag from Bel Bambini

August 06, 2024
blog

Salma Hayek is in the enviable position of having access to a world of fine leather bags. Fiance, and father of her child, Francois-Henri Pinault is the CEO of PPR, a French multinational holding company specializing in retail shops and luxury brands. The company owns Gucci and other subsidiaries including Yves Saint Laurent, Bottega Veneta, Alexander McQueen, Stella McCartney, and Balenciaga.

Nevertheless, while out shopping at celebrity favorite shop Bel Bambini recently, Salma picked up a leather tote for herself from the Born Out of Necessity collection. Salma purchased the large Bo2njour tote in Vintage Pink/Cherry ($335 – the bag is also available from the manufacturer for $299) These diaper/work totes may not boast quite the same brand recognition as a Gucci or Balenciaga in certain Hollywood circles, but they are a new mom’s best friend.

Born Out of Necessity bags are extremely well thought out and loaded with features such as a watertight mylar lined hot/cold compartment to keep food and beverages fresh, oversized easy-access side bottle pockets, an extremely well organized interior and a spacious shape that wears well close to the body and holds all you needs for a long day out with baby. The unique structured shape keeps the bag from collapsing when you set it down, which means that you can find what you want with one hand while balancing a toddler on your hip. The tote is available in large and small sizes.

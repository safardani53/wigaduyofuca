Everything to know about John Mayer's 2023 tour

February 28, 2024
blog

JOHN Mayer has been a household name for over twenty years since dropping his first album, Room For Squares, in 2001.

Now, John Mayer has released eight studio albums, and after touring with the Dead and Company, he's hitting the road again, this time solo.

John Mayer tour 2023: How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for John Mayer's solo tour are available as of February 1, 2023, for artist pre-sale and will open to the general public on February 3.

If you want to buy pre-sale tickets, head to John Mayer's website, which will prompt you to enter your name, email address, and phone number in exchange for a password.

You'll then be taken to Ticketmaster, where you will enter the password.

As of 5.30pm on February 1, 2023, tickets to his show are selling for anywhere from $400 to several thousand dollars.

What dates did Nickelback add to their tour and how can I get tickets?

May 10, 2024
blog

THE NICKELBACK bandmates are getting ready to embark on their 2023 'Get Rollin'' tour.

The Canadian rock band will travel across North America throughout the summer and fall of 2023.

What dates and locations did Nickelback add to their tour?

On Thursday, June 8, 2023, Nickelback took to Twitter to announce that they are adding 16 dates to their 'Get Rollin'" tour.

They tweeted, in part: "We’ve got some new dates coming your way!"

As per Blabbermouth.net, the additional dates and locations include:

  • August 29 at Freedom Mortgage Pavilion (Camden, NJ)
  • August 31 at PNC Bank Arts Center (Holmdel, NJ)
  • September 2 at Veterans United Home Loans Amphitheater (Virginia Beach, VA)
  • September 12 at PNC Music Pavilion (Charlotte, NC)
  • September 14 at Coastal Credit Union Music Park at Walnut Creek (Raleigh, NC)
  • September 16 at Oak Mountain Amphitheatre (Pelham, AL)
  • September 17 at Bank Plus Amphitheater (Southaven, MS)
  • September 19 at T-Mobile Center (Kansas City, MO)
  • September 21 at BOK Center (Tulsa, OK)
  • September 23 at Moody Center (Austin, TX)
  • September 25 at Simmons Bank Arena (Little Rock, AR)
  • September 27 at Thompson-Boiling Arena (Knoxville, TN)
  • September 29 at The Pavilion at Star Lake (Burgettstown, PA)
  • September 30 at Riverbend Music Center (Cincinnati, OH)
  • October 3 at Pinnacle Bank Arena (Lincoln, NE)
  • October 5 at FARGODOME (Fargo, ND)

Read More On Music Tours

The band will now visit 53 cities throughout the United States and Canada as part of their upcoming tour, which begins on June 12, 2023, in Québec City.

How can I get tickets to the Nickelback tour?

As Blabbermouth.net reported, tickets will be available via an artist presale on Friday, June 9, 2023, at 10am local time.

More presales will reportedly take place throughout Saturday, June 10 and Sunday, June 11.

The general sale will begin on Tuesday, June 13 at 10am local time.

You can get tickets via Live Nation and Ticketmaster and view VIP packages on Nickelback's website.

Why did Nickelback expand their tour?

Nickelback expanded their tour "due to incredible demand" as per Blabbermouth.net.

ET Canada described the demand as "overwhelming."

On November 18, 2022, the band released their tenth studio album, Get Rollin,' which inspired the upcoming tour.

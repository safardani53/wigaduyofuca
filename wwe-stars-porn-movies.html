ALTHOUGH many stars make a very lucrative career for themselves inside the wrestling ring, others have been forced to earn cash elsewhere.

Legends such as Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson and Dave Bautista are making plenty of dosh for themselves in Hollywood as actors on the silverscreen.

However, not everybody is as fortunate as them when it comes to their roles in movies.

In fact, a number of ex-WWE performers have turned to the porn industry to help bring in the cash with mixed success.

Sun Sport takes a look at ten wrestlers who have ventured into the adult entertainment industry:

10 BUFF BAGWELL

Buff ‘The Stuff’ was a huge hit with WCW during the Monday Night Wars before going on to endure a miserable stint in WWE.

He continued to wrestle for independent promotions before deciding on a new career as he admitted to being a full-time gigolo for a company named "Cowboy 4 Angels" - charging women $3000 a night.

Bagwell also penned a contract to work for adult entertainment company Vivid Entertainment.

9 BEULAH MCGILLICUTTY

Best remembered by ECW fans as the gorgeous valet to Raven and the childhood love of Tommy Dreamer.

Despite looking like a real sweetie, her naughty side was revealed when a video of her in another kind of action emerged online.

She later went on to pose in a number of saucy and explicit snaps but would go on to write and publish a children's book called “Gertrude the Great” in 2012.

8 BUBBA RAY DUDLEY

Yes that’s right, Bubba Ray Dudley has starred in a porn movie alongside the legendary Ron Jeremy.

The ECW and WWE legend became friends with adult film director Rob Black in the 90s, the husband of Lizzy Borden.

That friendship led to him fulfilling one of his ambitions to direct a porn movie under the guidance of Black.

And Bubba even got a clothed cameo in the movie titled “Whack Attack” that also starred Jeremy.

7 MICKIE JAMES

Mickie James is well-known to WWE fans after achieving legendary status inside the ring.

She also enjoyed huge success with rival promotion TNA.

But before making the breakthrough in the wrestling business she used to pose nude for a number of websites.

6 X-PAC

The WWE hall of famer was hugely popular in the late 90s but found himself released after the turn of the century.

That led to him turning to his wild side to remain relevant alongside his then fiancee Chyna.

The pair starred in a home-made adult movie called “One Night In China” and it actually proved to be really successful, winning several awards and remaining a best-selling adult film for a long time.

5 GANGREL

Best-known as the leader of The Brood during the Attitude Era, the vampire-like star was later fired due to being overweight.

That led to a dramatic career change as he became a director of adult films.

In 2007 he agreed a contract to direct 12 films for a company named “New P**n Order” under his Vampire Warrior ring name with his first movie titled “Miami Rump Shakerz 2”.

4 CHYNA

The late, great Ninth Wonder of the World was rightly inducted into WWE’s hall of fame last year.

Following her iconic wrestling career she decided to do that home-made movie with X-Pac.

After its success, Chyna later starred in a sequel to that and signed a deal with adult film moguls Vivid Video.

In total she appeared in three films for the company.

3 SHELLY MARTINEZ

After a short-lived career in WWE as part of ECW she went on to work for TNA.

But she enjoyed much more success away from the ring after becoming a pin-up model and starring in a softcore bondage video named “The Notorious Jewel De'Nyle & Shelly Martinez”.

Martinez would later pose nude for several magazines and she is now the official Hollywood correspondent for horror news website MoreHorror.com.

GEORGIA Harrison has revealed that she plans to sue Stephen Bear for destroying her career after her revenge porn hell.

"I’ve gone through two years of being dropped by every brand who ever worked with me. My career was thriving prior to it.”

Bear was sentenced to 21 months last week and refused to apologise to her for the distress he caused — or explain why he did it.

Next week, Georgia will talk about her ordeal in ITV2 documentary Revenge Porn: Georgia vs Bear, which she has been filming for over two years.

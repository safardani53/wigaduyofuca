For Brazilian-American actress Camila Mendes the sets of her movies hold a special place in her heart. Not only has she found fame through the graft she puts in the sets, but it has also given her with beautiful memories and relationships to cherish.

The Virginia native is one of the most happening actresses present today after finding fame through her portrayal of Veronica in the popular show Riverdale.

Her stars have been on the up ever since Riverdale and is currently busy with multiple projects. She starred in 2018's comedy melodrama The New Romantic and her two movies The Stand-In, and Coyote Lake is currently in post-production phases. Moreover, she also fronts up the brand IKEA.

Camila Mendes Bio(Age): Parents, Ethnicity, Nationality, Siblings

American national, Camila Carrao Mendes was born on June 29, 1994, in Charlottesville, Virginia. She graced into this world as the daughter of mother Gisele Carraro and father Victor Mendes. She has an elder sister named Kiara Moreno as a sibling in the family.

Camila, aged 24, possesses South American ethnicity as both of her parents are Brazilian--her father is from Brasilia and mother from Porto Alegre. Her mother has Italian roots while her father has Portuguese descent. She now identifies herself as Brazilian-American with Italian and Portuguese origin. Her parents separated when she was 8 of age.

Camila graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from NYU Tisch School of Arts. During her academic years, she was well recognized for her talent and craft and was sought by several filmmakers for casting.

Despite being relatively short in height, she has already made a fortune for herself. Standing at a height of 5 feet 2 inches she boasts a net worth which is estimated to be in the region of $3 million.

Camila Mendes' Boyfriends: Dated Both In And Out Of Showbiz 

The talented actress has always been on the limelight whether that be because of her fabulous acting skills or because of her dating history. She has been linked with plenty of names--with her boyfriends coming in and out of the showbiz. 

Gorgeous Camila first sparked a romance with someone in the showbiz.

Dating History: Camila and Ian pose together while dating (Photo: WhosDatedWho.com)

She was reported to be dating director, Ian Wallace in 2013. The pair were frequent in the LA party scene which sparked rumors about Ian being her boyfriend. The couple allegedly dated for about three years before separating in 2016.

The relationship left a sour note in her life, so much so that Camila had enough of dating someone from the industry. In an interview with Nylon, she stated that she would never ever couple again with someone from the industry:

"It’s just dating people in the industry is tough. I did for a little bit. It’s hard when that’s your world. You only meet people through work and that can be really tough, because you’re not necessarily meeting people that you’re similar to. It’s just people that you’re with because you’re working on the same project.

She particularly indicated her dislike in actors, stating:

I realize that I don’t think I like actors. Actors are really emotionally complicated. You would think they would be more in tune with their emotions, but sometimes they’re just not. I just really need to get out of this industry with someone who is in a stable environment.”

Sticking to her words, she next dated someone out of the showbiz.

Two years after her breakup with Wallace, she found love again with her childhood friend from Florida, Victor Houston. The pair reunited in New York after several years and started dating each other.

For Camilla, Victor was a breeze of fresh air and someone with whom she could relax and not think about the sets. With Victor having no ties whatsoever with Hollywood, she found solace with him.

She Is Dating Again--New Boyfriend Is Actor  

When you spend too much time in the showbiz industry, your life blends in with it too. Possibly it was the same for Mendes. Despite stating her words about not dating actors again, she failed to keep up with those words.

Having dated both from inside and outside the industry, Camila backtracked from her original comments about not dating men from Hollywood. 

She is currently in a relationship again and this time with her co-star, Charles Melton aka Reggie from the show Riverdale. They made their relationship official with a romantic Instagram post.

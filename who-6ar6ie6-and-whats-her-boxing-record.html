FAMOUS for her gothic makeup looks 6ar6ie6 is another influencer entering the world of boxing.

But how did she earn fame and what is her boxing record?

Who is 6ar6ie6?

6ar6ie6 (Barbie Three Six) also known as Avery Pongracz is an Australian boxer and TikTok influencer.

She was born on December 19, 2001, in Bentleigh, Australia.

Pongracz is best known for creating macabre lip sync videos on TikTok.

She is also known for her gothic mannequin makeup look.

As of May 2023, her Tiktok account has 2.5 million followers.

What's her boxing record?

6ar6ie6 had her debut fight against fitness influencer Whitney Johns on April 22, 2023, as part of the High Stakes Quarter Final by Kingpyn boxing at the OVO Arena in London, England.

After the end of round one it looked like Johns was knocked out.

However the referee allowed her to proceed to the next round after she got up stumbling.

In an interview after the fight 6ar6ie6 said: "I thought I had her knocked out!

"I thought I did it, but I didn't do it.

"Everyone doubted me, I said I could do it."

She won with a one-sided decision with the judges scoring it 50-45, 50-44 and 50-44 in favour of 6ar6ie6.

About her win she posted on Instagram: "It wasn’t pretty but i kept my promises.”

Due to visa issues she needed to cancel her debut in November 2022, against Fangs at the third Misfits series at the Moody Center in Austin, Texas.

When is 6ar6ie6 next fighting?

Her next fight is against Emily Brooke on July 1, 2023, as part of the High Stake Semi Finals by Kingpyn boxing in the 3Arena in Dublin, Ireland.

Brooke won the quarter finals against Amber O'Donnell.

Inside Peter Facinellis Adorable Proposal To Girlfriend Lily Anne Harrison

May 15, 2024
blog

Peter Facinelli proposed to his fiancée Lily Anne Harrison on New Year’s Eve in 2019, hoping for a “new year, a new beginning.” People reported on January 2, 2020, that the Twilight star proposed to his actor/writer girlfriend, Harrison, while on vacation in Mazatlan, Mexico. For the occasion, they were joined by Facinelli’s daughters from a previous marriage and Harrison’s parents.

The actor proposed during a romantic beach supper at the Estrella Del Mar Beach and Golf Resort, and the couple is planning to marry soon. However, due to the pandemic, the couple has had to postpone their wedding plans.

Peter Facinelli on His Wedding with Fiancée Lily Anne Harrison

Facinelli stated he didn’t have a date since he didn’t know when the pandemic would finish in People’s April 1, 2021 edition. In the wedding arrangements, the actor deferred to his fiancée, Harrison.

“I’m sure I’d like to be a part of it,” he said, “but I believe it’s more important to me that she’s happy and that it’s her special day.”
“I’d like to get married on a beach somewhere,” the actor expressed his desire.

There was no way a pandemic wedding could happen, according to Facinelli’s description of the event.
On October 20, 2021, Nurse Jackie alum spoke with People about the couple’s marriage again.

“I want [our wedding] to take place in a world without masks,” Facinelli remarked. The actor, on the other hand, stated that they were not in a hurry. He continued, “We’re devoted to each other and we’re enjoying a life together.”

Even though the actor stated that he intended to marry in 2022, “I’m already married. Except for the piece of paper and the celebration, we have everything!” There have been no reports concerning their big day as of 2022.

Peter Facinelli’s Failed Marriage to Ex Jennie Garth

From 2001 to 2013, Facinelli was married to actress Jennie Garth for 12 years. In 2012, the former couple startled fans by announcing their split.

Luca, 24, Lola, 19, and Fiona, 15, were their three daughters. For the sake of their three girls, they were resolved to co-parent successfully following their divorce.
The exes have maintained a positive friendship throughout the years, and in 2017 they were photographed vacationing in Cancun, Mexico, with their three children. Harrison Ford and Garth Abrams’ husband, Dave Abrams, were also present.

The Thirteen Minutes singer recently spoke with ET Canada about his broken marriage to ex-wife Garth and how he’s safeguarding his relationship with fiancée Harrison.

According to Facinelli, the start of any relationship is fantastic, but things go awry when unhealthy habits develop.

“It becomes ingrained and grows, and you become resentful when you try to break those habits,” he explained.

In light of this, the actor said to his fiancée that he informed her, “This is something I truly want to keep. I want us to be extremely cautious of one another, and let’s work hard every day to avoid developing harmful habits.” As of 2022, Facinelli and Harrison have been dating for about six years.

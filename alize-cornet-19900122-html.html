In 2014, Cornet is known for beating world No.1 Serena Williams three times. At the Dubai Tennis Championships, she scored a line against Williams in the semi-finals, helping Cornet reach his biggest single-shot final in nearly six years. In July at the Wimbledon Championship, she came back from a loss to beat Williams in the third round, leaving the American eliminated at the earliest since 2005.

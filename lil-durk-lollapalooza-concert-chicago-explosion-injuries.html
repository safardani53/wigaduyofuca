DRAMATIC footage shows the horror moment Lil Durk was struck by an explosion while performing at Lollapalooza.

The rapper, 29, suffered serious facial injuries following the scary incident at the Chicago festival on July 30.

He was performing the track Back in Blood when pyrotechnics appeared to explode in front of him.

Lil Durk, whose real name is Durk Derrick Banks, stopped rapping when the explosion happened and staggered backward.

He was hit by several blasts and footage shows white smoke billowing across the stage.

A DJ can be heard saying: “Whoa. You alright bro?” as the rapper covered his eyes with his t-shirt.

Lil Durk later said: “F**k it, no more smoke, let’s do it.”

He later took to Instagram, explaining that he finished his performance for his fans.

Lil Durk has since shared a picture of him in hospital wearing a white face mask and a patch covering his right eye.

He has since revealed that he’s taking a break to focus on his health.

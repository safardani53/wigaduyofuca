CHEICK DOUCOURE was screaming in pain as he was stretchered off during Crystal Palace's defeat to Luton Town.

The midfielder was the second Palace player to pick up an injury during the game as Eberechi Eze was also forced off.

Doucoure went down after kicking the ball and was left writhing in pain at Kenilworth Road.

As he was stretchered off he had his head in his hands as he appeared in huge discomfort.

He was replaced by Will Hughes, who was greeted with boos by the Luton crowd for his links to Watford.

And Palace fans were less than impressed with many calling for the season to be over, especially with their side losing 2-1.

Naomie Olindo is a French reality star best known for starring in the Bravo hit television series, Southern Charm. She appeared in the show alongside her ex-boyfriend Craig Conover. She is also an entrepreneur and businesswoman and runs her own clothing line called L’Abeye.

Early Life, Family, & Education

Naomie Olindo was born on August 4, 1992, in France, to father Joel Olindo and mother Carole Olindo. It's unknown it the TV star has a sibling or not.

Her parents own restaurants in Charleston, USA, and France. She is of French nationality and belongs to white ethnicity.

As for her education, Naomie has attended the College of Charleston where she received a Master degree in Business Administration. She has also majored in Finance.

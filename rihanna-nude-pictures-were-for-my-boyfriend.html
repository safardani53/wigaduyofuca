Nude Pictures Were For My Boyfriend

February 13, 2024
blog

Who can forget those “alleged” Rihanna nude pictures that surfaced in May? Actually, they were pretty forgettable, even though they sure appeared to be her.

She hasn’t commented on them one way or the other – until now. Rihanna confirmed to Hot 97 that it was really her in those nude photos “someone” leaked.

The intended recipient? Her boyfriend “at the time.”

“They were for my boyfriend at the time,” the singer told the station, adding that “if you don’t send your boyfriend naked pictures, then I feel bad for him.”

That’s great advice to all the girls out there. If you don’t take nude pics of yourself, and email them to your boyfriend, you obviously don’t care about him.

Rihanna is pro-naked pics. And weird hair. [Photo: PacificCoastNewsOnline.com]

Rihanna does admit she was embarrassed by the nude pic leak: “I thought, ‘Oh great, so now there’s nothing they don’t know about me and my private life.’”

Just the same, she doesn’t say she regrets taking them, just that they got out. She was just doing her romantic duty. Making mom blush? Collateral damage.

“It was humiliating and it was embarrassing, especially my mom having to see that,” she continued, adding that she sent flowers before breaking the news.

“I was nervous [but] when the world is against me, she’s always there.”

The same can’t be said for Chris Brown – the presumed recipient, but who has denied leaking the photos, and hasn’t said if they were even sent to him.

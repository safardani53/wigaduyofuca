Amanda Holden, 52, wows in red dress that channels animated beauty Jessica Rabbit

February 19, 2024

TELLY and radio favourite Amanda Holden gets in toon with Christmas by wearing a clinging red dress that channels Jessica Rabbit.

The 52-year-old went for the look made famous in 1988’s animated classic Who Framed Roger Rabbit as she unveiled Baylis & Harding’s Yuletide campaign.

The Britain’s Got Talent judge said: “Christmas can never come soon enough for me each year and we’re already playing the music in our house.

“I’m so excited to be Baylis & Harding’s Christmas Ambassador and featuring in their festive advert.”

She sounds so animated.

Amanda revealed her take on nerdy Scooby-Doo icon Velma for Halloween.

She slipped into an orange long-sleeve crop top, red PVC mini skirt and thigh-high socks.

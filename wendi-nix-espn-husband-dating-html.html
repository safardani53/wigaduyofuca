Wendi Nix’s Bio: Age, Height

The ESPN anchor was born on 17th September 1974, in South Carolina, United States.

Nix got her passion for sports as a child from her beloved father, Wayne Nix, a former football and athletic coach. 

She may not be an athlete herself, but her love for sports was imprinted on her from her childhood as she used to play many games growing up.

Enrolled at Wofford College, She graduated in 1996 with a degree in French and Economics. A year later, she earned her master's degree in sports management from the University of Massachusetts.

Standing tall at the height of 5 feet and 10 inches, the 44-year-old has a stunning physique with blonde hair and blue eyes.

Wendi Nix’s Career

Before working for ESPN, Nix was a sports anchor and reporter at WHDH-TV (NBC) in Boston for three years (2003-06). She also worked for FOX Sports and New England Sports Network.

Nix began her career at ESPN from 2006 as a TV host, covering college and pro football games.

In 2009, she became the first woman to host the Walter Camp Football Foundation All-American Awards dinner in 2009.

Additionally, Nix co-hosted Sunday NFL Countdown for three complete seasons and was the host of the weekly Chalk Talk events for ESPN’s prime-time Monday Night Football.

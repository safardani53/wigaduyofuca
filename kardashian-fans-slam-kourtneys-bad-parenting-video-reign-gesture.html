Kardashian fans slam Kourtney's 'bad parenting' after photos show son Reign, 7, giving NSFW gesture

KOURTNEY Kardashian has received backlash after photos show son Reign giving a NSFW gesture.

The Hulu star's youngest child Reign, seven, threw up middle fingers in various snaps.

In a post shared on a popular Kardashians fan Instagram account, Reign shocked fans.

The two photos show a long-haired Reign from 2019 and the other, a more recent snap, of the youngster out running errands with Kourtney, 43, and her husband Travis Barker, 46.

Reign is flipping off the camera in both photos.

The post, captioned "Reign is hilarious," quickly garnered backlash from fans for the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum's parenting style in the comment section."

CUTTING UP

Recently, Reign seemed to misbehave while at a restaurant opening by making the same shocking gesture to the cameras.

Reign appeared to have had enough with photographers at the Los Angeles event.

The little one accompanied his mother and step-father to the grand opening of the vegan eatery, Crossroads Kitchen in Calabasas, California, on Thursday.

Travis is an investor in the upscale restaurant owned by legendary chef Tal Ronnen, who also has a location in West Hollywood.

The Blink-182 drummer shared photos on his Instagram Stories of the ribbon-cutting ceremony, where Reign stood at the front of the unveiling.

In the snaps, Kourtney and Travis watched Reign cut into the ribbon with scissors to signify the restaurant's official opening.

However, one photo circulated capturing a different portrayal of the evening from Reign's eyes.

In the pic, Reign and his sister Penelope were standing in the restaurant's patio area while Travis was talking to a woman seemingly involved in the project.

Reign appeared distracted by cameras snapping their picture from afar as he leaned over a railing and flashed his middle fingers.

Travis didn't appear to see the mishap, and Kourtney was nowhere in sight.

SLEEPING ARRANGEMENT

Earlier this month, Kourtney received mixed reviews after she revealed she still co-sleeps with her daughter.

Kourtney made the admission while appearing on Amanda Hirsch's Not Skinny But Not Fat podcast.

“She’s slept with me every day since she was born and pretty much still does,” the TV star said.

“Unless she has a friend sleep over or unless she leaves me to go sleep at Travis’ or Auntie Coco’s [Khloe Kardashian] or Auntie Kiki’s [Kim Kardashian]. But besides that, we are so close.”

Amanda praised Kourtney for being a "very all-in" mother who “doesn’t follow any of the rules that [she’s] supposed to."

Kourtney agreed and stressed how similar she is to her "mini-me," Penelope.

"I can't even explain it. She is my girl that I do not worry about," the reality star said.

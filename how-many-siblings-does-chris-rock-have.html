How many siblings does Chris Rock have?

CHRIS Rock has made a name for himself as a stand-up comedian.

Some of his siblings are closely following in his footsteps.

How many siblings does Chris Rock have?

Chris Rock is the eldest of seven siblings - six younger brothers and one younger sister.

Chris' younger brothers are - Tony Rock, Charles Rock, Kenny Rock, Andre Rock, Jordan Rock, and Brian Rock.

Chris' only sister is called Andi Rock.

Among all his siblings, Tony Rock seems to be the next most popular entertainer in the family.

Who is Chris Rock’s brother Tony Rock?

Tony Rock was born on June 30, 1974, in Brooklyn, New York.

Just like his brother Chris, Tony is also a stand-up comedian and actor by profession.

Tony has appeared on several radio and television segments, including The Howard Stern Show and The D'Angelo Show.

Tony is popularly known for playing the role of Uncle Ryan in the sitcom, Everybody Hates Chris. He also starred in All of Us.

From 2008 to 2009, the comic starred in his personal sketch comedy series, The Tony Rock Project.

Featured on an episode of Comedy Central Presents, Tony also had a role in the film, Think Like a Man.

Currently, Tony is a judge on the new Netflix baking competition Is It Cake?, which first premiered in March of 2022.

What is Chris Rock's net worth?

As of April 2022, Chris Rock has an estimated net worth of about $60million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

His source of wealth is said to be from his career in stand-up comedy.

He has also earned a lot of income from movies he has acted in, and books he has written.

ACHRAF HAKIMI is a wanted man.

The Inter Milan starlet, 22,  is a transfer target for both Arsenal and Chelsea.

The Blues are said to have offered up five players in a bid to tempt the Italians into a £69million swap deal.

And while his professional life is thriving, he isn't doing badly in his personal one either.

'WORLD'S MOST BEAUTIFUL ACTRESS'

Hakimi is married to the "world's most beautiful actress" - who is 12 years older than him.

Hiba Abouk, 34, who is of Spanish Tunisian descent, is best known for playing the role of Fatima in popular Spanish TV show El Principe.

Born in Madrid, she is the youngest of four siblings and loves flamenco and languages. She can speak Spanish, Arabic, French, English and Italian.

Her acting career took off in 2010 when she starred in the comedy series La isla de los nominados on TV channel Cuatro.

The following year she was cast in a Spanish adaptation of Cheers.

AWARD WINNER

But it was in 2014 she hit the limelight with her debut in El Principe, a crime drama series watched by more than five million viewers - and in which she plays the lead role.

That same year she was presented with the Women's Beauty Icon gong at the Cosmopolitan Beauty Awards.

Hiba has called Achraf "the man of my life" and even admitted thinking about being his bride since they went public with their relationship in 2018.

WELCOMED A BABY

In  October 2020, Hakimi announced on Instagram that Abouk was expecting their first child.

“Thanks Hiba for making me feel the best feeling that can exist, and for being my life partner and my best friend! I love you!” he wrote after announcing the pregnancy.

And then in February last year their son Amin was born.

The 2019 Young Arab Player of the Year has had a remarkable life so far. Will he be ready to take on the adventure of a move to Premier League with his family?

Jalen Ramsey’s Daughters

As of 2023, Jalen Ramsey has two daughters, Breelyn Erin and Brooklyn.

Breelyn is his first daughter, born on July 16, 2018, while Brooklyn, his second daughter, was born on September 27, 2019. 

Breelyn Erin

When Breelyn was born in 2018, Jalen was reporting for his training camp with the Jaguars. Later, he took his leave and missed four practice sessions to spend family time in Tennessee.

The cornerback spoke about his first child’s expectations during the interview with Eastbay. He said his daughter would be a track and field star because he and Breanna excelled in the sport in high school and college.

Later, Jalen announced the birth of his first child through Twitter, writing, “For me, family is among the most important things in my life, along with faith and football. Today starts a new chapter in my life, as Bre and I are blessed to welcome our baby into this world.”

After his return to training camp, Jalen frequently talked about his daughter. He stated that he fed her while his ex-girlfriend, Breanna Tate, would change her diapers. 

Brooklyn

On September 27, 2019, Jalen and Breanna welcomed their second daughter, Brooklyn. After the news, Jalen updated his Twitter account bio as “Dada to Queens Breelyn & Brooklyn.”

The football cornerback was on leave due to a sore back before flying to Nashville for the birth of his second child. 

Early Life & Relationship 

Jalen Ramsey was born on October 24, 1994, in Smyrna, Tennessee, to supporting parents Lamont Ramsey and Margie Ramsey. He spent his childhood with his older brother named Jamal Ramsey. 

Ramsey attended the Battle Ground Academy in Franklin, Tennessee, where he played basketball, track, and football. Following it, he enrolled at Ensworth High School University in Nashville, Tennessee. 

He initially committed to the University of Southern California but later changed to Florida State University.

Jacksonville Jaguars drafted Jalen as the 5th overall pick in the 2016 NFL Draft.

Today, he is the cornerback for the Los Angeles Rams of the NFL. His contract extension with Rams in 2020 made him the highest-paid defensive back in league history.

Who Is Breanna Tate?

After high school, Breanna attended the University of Mississippi. She and her sister joined the track squad. However, she wasn’t much involved in the race track after graduation.

When did I Am Legend take place?

I Am Legend is set in the year 2012 and is based on the 1954 novel of the same name by Richard Matheson.

A virus that was created for the purpose of battling cancer and it's administered to those with cancer, and it looked promising.

But a few years later, it mutated into plague that kills most of the people on Earth or turns them into creatures and there are a few who are immune.

Amongst them is military scientist Robert Neville (Will Smith), who has spent the last three years after the plague decimated humanity trying to find a cure.

What city does I Am Legend take place in?

Like a lot of films, the movie is set in New York, although in the book it is set in Los Angeles.

Producer Akiva Goldsman decided to move the story from LA to the Big Apple to take advantage of locations that would more easily show emptiness.

Production began in 2006 in NYC, filming mainly on location in the city, including a $5million scene at the Brooklyn Bridge.

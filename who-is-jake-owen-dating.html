Jake Owen Biography

Jake Owen was born on a Friday, August 28, 1981 in Winter Haven, FL. His birth name is Joshua Ryan Owen and he is currently 42 years old. People born on August 28 fall under the zodiac sign of Virgo. His zodiac animal is Rooster.

Joshua Ryan “Jake” Owen is an American country music artist. Signed to RCA Nashville in 2006, he released his debut album Startin’ with Me that year. This album produced three singles, all of which reached Top 20 on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart: his debut single “Yee Haw”, “Startin’ with Me”, and “Something About a Woman”. A second album, 2009’s Easy Does It, has accounted for three more singles: “Don’t Think I Can’t Love You”, “Eight Second Ride”, and “Tell Me”. In September 2011, Owen achieved his first number-one hit on the country charts with the title track to his third album Barefoot Blue Jean Night, as did with “Alone with You”, “The One That Got Away”, and “Anywhere with You”. His fourth album, Days of Gold produced two singles with its title track, which broke the Top 20 in 2014, and the number one single “Beachin'”.

Owen started off pursuing a career in professional golfing. He suffered a wakeboarding accident while at Florida State University, which ended his athletic career.

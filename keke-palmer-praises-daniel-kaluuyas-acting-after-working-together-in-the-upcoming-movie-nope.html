After shooting for the next new horror movie, Keke and Daniel are getting to know one other better. Keke Palmer said in an interview:

“I like Daniel and have always wanted to collaborate with him.” It’s one thing to say, “Oh my god, I’m going to be in Jordan Peele’s movie, but then I’m going to be in Jordan Peele’s movie alongside Daniel!”

“He’s extremely excellent, like really good”: Daniel has received nothing but praise from Keke Palmer. During her interview with PEOPLE. Keke Palmer also said that she has always admired Kaluuya before seeing him in Get Out. She declared:

“When I watched his episode of Black Mirror, I thought he was incredibly amazing, like really good. Acting acting acting So when Get Out came out, I knew the movie was going to be excellent because they got the dude from the Black Mirror episode!

As close friends on this occasion, Keke appreciated Daniel as a person, not only as an actor or co-star. When asked about his accent, she stated:

“It was an honor to work with him. It’s strange because I picked up on some of his personality while we were working, but I really picked up on his personality when he was free to utilize his accent. On set, he spoke with an American accent most of the time, which unfortunately dulls a little bit of your personality because you’re practically in another language, but now that we’re traveling around, doing press together, and hanging out, it’s like, ‘Oh, I get to truly see the way that you are.'”

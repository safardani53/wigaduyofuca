Furious Sky Sports presenter shares pic of womans bare feet on train seat and blasts so wrong

blog

A SKY SPORTS presenter has tweeted a photo of a passenger putting her bare feet on a train seat.

Faye Carruthers blasted the woman as "so wrong on so many levels" after being forced to move seats on a Thameslink service.

The sports broadcaster shared the pic with more than 25,000 followers, who branded the unknown passenger an "absolute savage", "riff raff" and "gross".

Faye, who works for Soccer Saturday, talkSPORT and IMG, posted the image showing a woman with no socks or shoes plonking her feet on the seat cushion.

A pair of trainers can be seen placed underneath the woman's seat as she parks her feet on the empty seat.

A newspaper and a rucksack are just a few centimetres away from her bare feet, which caused the journalist to share her disgust.

'JUST GROSS'

Ms Carruthers wrote: "So wrong on so many levels... #people."

She then tweeted: "Everyone thinking it’s a bloke - it’s a woman - was v tempted to say something and then just moved."

Some of her followers piled in to express their horror on Twitter.

One wrote: "I’d whack them with his newspaper that’s just gross."

Another said: "Why dont they put a sign up saying Not allowed, fine them and encourage others to report it. Some people are so full of themselves. Sheesh."

A third wrote: "Soooooo wrong ffs."

But one person said: "It does look weird but be honest if it was summer, they were wearing shorts and just slipped off a pair of flip flops I bet you’d be fine with it.".

And another claimed: "She probably thinks she’s being polite by not putting her shoes on the seat!"

Ms Carruthers has also worked with Sky Sports News, Premier League Production, the Guardian’s Football Weekly Podcast, Absolute Radio and BBC Radio 5Live.

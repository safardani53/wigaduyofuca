Ariana DeBose is seen arriving at a dinner honoring her and her work ahead of the 95th Academy Awards. The intimate event was held at a private residence in West Hollywood, California on Thursday, March 9.

This year’s awards season has been a true celebration of style – and OMEGA watches have been a popular choice on every red carpet. With the 95th Academy Awards taking place this Sunday, the Swiss brand has hosted a special season-ending event alongside ambassador Ariana DeBose.

The evening was hosted by Arnaud Michon, OMEGA US Brand President & CEO of Swatch Group, at a private residence in Los Angeles. Guests enjoyed cocktails and dinner while honoring the OMEGA styles that have been worn in 2023.

Ariana DeBose was an Oscar winner in 2022 for her performance in West Side Story and was delighted to be joining OMEGA on Thursday’s celebration. She said, “Winning an Academy Award was a literal dream come true, and I’m beyond humbled to return to the Oscars this year and celebrate the incredible work in our industry over the past year. Omega has been an amazing partner since I began this journey, and I’m so grateful to them for continuing to champion me and my work.”

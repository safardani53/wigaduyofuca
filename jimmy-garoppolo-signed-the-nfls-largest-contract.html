Jimmy Garoppolo has been the subject of numerous trade rumors since 2017. He was linked to trades with the Chicago Bears and Cleveland Browns, but he stayed with the Patriots for the first half of the 2017 season until joining the San Francisco 49ers in October. In February 2018, he agreed to a five-year, $137.5 million deal with San Francisco, the richest in NFL history. Because of his high school experience as a linebacker and quarterback, American footballer Jimmy Garoppolo was able to finish his three-and-a-half-year quarterback apprenticeship with the New England Patriots under Tom Brady. He was traded to the San Francisco 49ers in 2017 to play quarterback. On September 23, 2018, while playing for the 49ers, he ended with 251 passing yards against the Kansas City Chiefs before departing the game with a left knee injury.

Jimmy Garoppolo Bio, Ethnicity, Parents, and Siblings

Jimmy Garoppolo was born on November 2, 1991, in Arlington Heights, Illinois, as James Richard Garoppolo. As a result, he was born in the United States. His father, Anthony J. (Tony) Garoppolo, and mother, Denise F. (Malec) Garoppolo, are his parents. Mike, Tony Jr., and Billy Garoppolo are his three brothers. Denise Garoppolo, a chef, and her husband Tony Garoppolo, an electrician, reared their children in a Chicago suburb.

Jimmy graduated from Rolling Meadows High School in Illinois, where he was a quarterback and linebacker for the Mustangs football team. He then went on to Eastern Illinois University, where he majored in management. From 2010 to 2013, he was a member of the Eastern Illinois Panthers. During his time at the university, he received multiple accolades and broke Tony Romo’s school record for career pass completions.

Jimmy Garoppolo Celebrates His Multiracial Identity

Jimmy Garoppolo is of mixed ancestry, having German, Italian, and Polish ancestors. Rose and Anthony Garoppolo, his paternal grandparents, were Italian immigrants, while his maternal grandparents, Harriet D. Seidel and Theodore J. Malec, were German and Polish.

Jimmy Garoppolo’s Relationship History

Jimmy is still in the news for reasons other than his games and injuries. He is also known for his personal life and girlfriends. He made headlines for his relationship with Alexandra Rose King, an Instagram sensation and model, when photos of them holding hands appeared on the internet. However, in an interview with Bleacher Report, Jimmy said that he was unaware of the purported relationship until his pals brought it up.

Shortly after, the sports and entertainment media were inundated with reports about Jimmy’s relationship with pornographic actress Kiara Mia. In July 2018, photographs and videos of the two eating at a high-end Greek restaurant leaked on the internet. Some fans and pundits chastised him for dating an adult celebrity. However, several of his superiors and coworkers backed him, claiming that his romantic life had no effect on his job or team. Jimmy did not deny or confirm his connection with Kiara, but he did say in an interview that he would be more cautious about revealing his personal life in public in the future.

My life away from the field — Even on social media, I’ve never been one to make things particularly public. I don’t spend a lot of time on there. But my life has been seen in a new light.

The 24 Best Amazon Holiday Decor Finds of 2023

August 04, 2024
blog

Amazon boxes are almost a staple on the porch during the holiday season, but they are extra special when they contain actual holiday decor. There are so many festive options from this massive online retailer hidden in secret corners within the site.

Best of all, Amazon is known for its super-fast shipping, so you still have plenty of time to deck your halls. From indoor decorations like artificial trees and stockings to porch welcome signs, there is something for every home, list, and style among our top Amazon holiday decor picks.

We searched every corner of Amazon for the best holiday decor available, and these are our favorite finds.

LoveinUSA Bottle Brush Trees with Topper Ornaments

Amazon

This set of nine bottlebrush trees is perfect for setting up your own holiday display without spending a ton of money. You can add these adorable trees to your mantel, put them on your desk at work, or adorn any other small table or space. You can even use them as gifts to family and friends or include them in a holiday diorama. With three sizes ranging from 4 to 6.9 inches tall, they are small enough to fit just about anywhere but plentiful enough to make a fun statement when gathered in a mini forest.

Ansulen Personalized Wire Stocking Name Tags

Amazon

Add a personalized look to your holiday stockings with these wire name tags. They’re also perfect for topping gifts or as ornaments for the tree. Since they’re a custom item, they’ll take a few days to ship, so order soon if you want them in time for holiday gifting and decorating.

Homesick The Nutcracker Candle

Homesick

With notes of sugar, orange, cloves, and a fireside, this candle will have sugar plum fairies dancing throughout your home. It brings all of the nostalgia that Homesick candles are known for and puts a holiday spin on the soy wax blend. Other scents for the holidays include Winter Mantel, Snow Day, and Log Cabin. The burn time is 60 to 80 hours. Depending on how often you burn candles, you can breeze through that in just a week, or it can last you all season.

Artoid Mode Buffalo Plaid Christmas Trees Table Runner

Amazon

Make your table festive and jolly for your family holiday meal with a table runner celebrating the season. This table runner has buffalo check trees, a "Merry Christmas" greeting, and a matching buffalo check border that runs the length of the runner. It measures 13 inches across and 72 inches long and is ideal for rectangular tables.

DII Oversized Christmas Dish Towels Set

Amazon

Your holiday decor doesn't have to be limited to your living room or family room, these 100-percent cotton dish towels are a bright and festive bit of decor you can add to your kitchen. The set includes one white towel and one red towel, and each one measures 28 x 18 inches. The towels are sturdy and machine washable, so they’ll last for many holidays to come. In addition to decorating your own home, these holiday dish towels are a great gift to bring to a holiday dinner or to wrap a plate of cookies to bring to a friend or neighbor.

Comenzar Flickering Candles

Amazon

If you want the beauty of flickering candlelight without worrying about a fire hazard, these battery-operated faux candles are just what you need. Nestle them into your greenery or other holiday displays for a rustic look with peace of mind. Each candle runs on three AAA batteries and can be controlled via a remote control. You can also use the remote to set a timer or dim the lights. You can put them up during the holidays and leave them up all through the winter.

Avoin Colorlife Christmas Throw Pillows

Amazon

If you're looking to give your bed or sofa some holiday style, consider these throw pillow covers for an easy seasonal upgrade. The 18x18-inch pillow covers offer four different designs, including classic Christmas characters and ornaments hanging from a tree limb, and feature nontraditional hues like pink and navy. All you need are pillow inserts to complete the look.

Soaoo Wooden Nutcracker Figures

Amazon

Create a festive display with this set of wooden nutcracker figures. Each figure is dressed in traditional holiday hues while holding mini figures like cupcakes, presents, and gingerbread men. Plus, the adorable wooden nutcracker has a lever on its back to open its mouth.

Lpusa Spiral Candles

Amazon

Perfect for outfitting holiday candleholders, these 7.5-inch taper candles feature a trendy spiral design. Choose from 30 different colors, including ivory, red and green, and more to dress up your seasonal tablescape or mantel.

A Treasury of Christmas Stories and Songs

Amazon

Dress up your coffee table with this festive and beautiful book filled with nostalgic and well-known songs and stories, including “Jingle Bells,” “Silent Night,” and “The Nutcracker.” Kids and adults alike will love thumbing through the colorful, glossy pages throughout the season.

Heyhouse Christmas Stockings Set

Amazon

This set of four stockings is perfect for a family, a group of friends, or one person who just loves getting small gifts. They come in a Christmas-themed color — burgundy — and have a cozy cable-knit pattern. These stockings are simple and classic, although you can easily jazz them up by hanging a themed or personalized ornament on the top.

Best Stocking Holder

Lulu Decor Cast Iron Stocking Holders

Amazon

This pair of cast iron stocking holders will be part of your holiday decor for years to come. They can be purchased in quantities ranging from two to eight, and you can customize the color of each of the Christmas trees. Each heavy-duty stocking holder weighs 6.3 pounds, so they can handle the stockings once they’re full of goodies without tipping over.

Boao Christmas Felt Garland

Amazon

Deck the windows, mantel, or tree with this colorful and festive felt light bulb garland. Each garland is 8.2 feet in length and can be displayed anywhere you want a little more holiday cheer during the season.

National Tree Company Kingswood Fir Slim Artificial Christmas Tree

Amazon

Nothing says "home for the holidays" like a beautifully decorated tree. This artificial Christmas tree from the National Tree Company is affordable, stands 7.5 feet tall, and can work in most spaces. Since it doesn’t have any decorations, you can add your own flair to express your traditions.

Kurt Adler Natural Capiz Lighted Tree Topper

Amazon

If you’re putting up a Christmas tree then you’ll definitely want to add a tree topper. This gold star from Kurt Adler will add a beautiful finishing touch to your decorated tree. The seven-point capiz star has a coil base to easily attach it to the tree with small bulbs at each point to illuminate it from behind.

Aiseno Christmas Tree Plush Skirt

Amazon

Enjoy a soft, plush place to nestle your presents under the tree. This tree skirt comes in four sizes and four colors, so you can match it to your decor and ornaments. It's secured by Velcro when you wrap it around the tree to stay in place, and it's double-layered so only the top layer has the plush faux fur. The bottom layer is smooth so that it lays in place.

Rocinha Rattan Christmas Tree Collar

Amazon

This hand-weaved collar will add a rustic and farmhouse feel to your Christmas tree setup. The dimensions can accommodate most live and artificial tree stands, so it’s a great alternative to the traditional tree skirt if you’re looking to make a statement in your living room.

Rimobul Woven Vinyl Non-Slip Placemats Set

Amazon

Even if you don’t get a white Christmas, these snowflake placemats still set the winter holiday mood at your table. They are made of non-slip vinyl and will stay in place before and after you cover them with plates full of delicious food. They come unfolded and can be stored flat to ensure that the placemats don’t wrinkle. The sturdy vinyl is also waterproof and wipeable.

Milltown Merchants Ceramic Christmas Tree

Amazon

A modern take on the nostalgic ceramic Christmas tree, this cactus sits in a pretty striped pot and lights up with a variety of colorful bulbs. It’s perfect for adding holiday cheer to your workspace or kitchen.

Ccinee Christmas Window Clings

Amazon

If you're looking for a budget-friendly, easy-to-use item to decorate for the holidays, these window clings are pretty fun. This set comes with over 167 pieces, including Santa, snowflakes, reindeer, and other holiday motifs. These make great activities for younger children as well as easy decor for office windows, classrooms, and other spaces that just need an extra dose of holiday cheer.

Calloway Mills Christmas Fun Doormat

Amazon

This festive doormat is durable and made of coir fiber using parts of coconut husks. This means that it can hold up in the rain and snow while also being rough enough to scrape your shoes clean before coming into the house. The underside is vinyl which keeps it from sliding around, even when the ground is wet. It comes in three sizes, so even if you have a large front porch, you can find a mat that's right for you.

RoseCraft Interchangeable Seasonal Welcome Sign Wreath

Amazon

This wreath is the perfect way to welcome people to your holiday-ready home. It says precisely that right on the front and features a removable decoration in place of the "O" in "home" that you can customize to celebrate different holidays and seasons. Some included customizations are event-specific, such as those for Halloween, Christmas, or Easter, while others are generally applicable to the seasons, such as a snowman for winter, falling leaves for autumn, and a flower for spring.

Glitzhome Christmas Wooden Welcome Sign

Amazon

The wooden porch sign trend gets a festive touch with this "Believe" Santa Claus. It comes in other holiday designs as well, such as a penguin, nutcracker, and snowman, but the Santa version invites people to believe in the magic of Christmas.

Alpine Corporation Rattan Grazing Reindeer

Amazon

This reindeer yard decoration looks great in the daytime and the nighttime, thanks to the rustic-looking rattan construction and the 50 halogen bulbs that light up at night. It comes with ground stakes, two replacement bulbs, and one replacement fuse so that it won’t tip over or lose lights. Even if it sustains some damage or wear, you can replace the broken bulbs and keep enjoying the decoration for years to come.

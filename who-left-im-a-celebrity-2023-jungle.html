I'M A Celebrity 2023 is drawing to a close, with the final now upon us.

For the last week, Ant and Dec have announced which celebrities would be leaving the jungle, and missing out on the grand finale.

Who left I'm a Celebrity last night?

On December 9, 2023, the This Morning host Josie Gibson was evicted from the Jungle, following former JLS singer Marvin Humes on December 8, 2023.

The TV legend narrowly missed her chance to participate in the I'm a Celebrity finale when ITV viewers voted to save Nigel Farage, Sam Thompson and Tony Bellew.

Speaking to I'm A Celebrity hosts Ant and Dec, Josie said: "I'm just so happy and going to be able to see my little boy. It's been a long time, so long."

When discussing her battle over cooking with campmate Fred Siriex, she said: "It was a battle of the chefs wasn't it. I think I put him straight in the end.

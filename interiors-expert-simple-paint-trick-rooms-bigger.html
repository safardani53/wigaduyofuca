Im an interiors expert, theres a simple paint trick that makes your rooms look bigger

blog

WANT to make your home feel bigger? You may only need a lick of paint. 

TikTok user @honeybeeandhollie shared her clever decorating trick which makes ceilings appear higher and rooms larger.

In a video, she showed how she paints one wall in a room with a statement colour, and then carries this onto a small part of the ceiling. 

She also adds it onto the adjacent walls to make an alcove effect.

This hack gives the impression that the wall is much larger than it looks.

She explained: “Use paint to create the illusion of height.”

Her video has racked up over 5,000 likes, with one person writing: “Love this idea, it looks like an alcove, think I’m going to try this.”

The interiors expert replied: “It actually creates a wee cosy space right above your bed. 

“I love it when I’m reading in bed. You should definitely try it. So easy.”

Another person wrote: “Oh that's cool I wanna try this.”

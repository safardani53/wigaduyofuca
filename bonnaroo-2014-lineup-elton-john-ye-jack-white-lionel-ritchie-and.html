Elton John, 'Ye, Jack White, Lionel Ritchie &amp; More!

April 10, 2024
blog

The legendary Elton John has received top-billing for the 2014 Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival, often referred to simply as Bonnaroo, this summer.

Other headliners at the four-day event in Manchester, Tenn.: Kanye West, Jack White, Lionel Ritchie, Vampire Weekend, the Avette Brothers and Phoenix.

We imagine not even a man with the ego the size of Mr. Kim Kardashian can complain about Sir Elton John’s name appearing first on the Bonnaroo bill.

Bonnaroo 2014 Lineup

VIEW GALLERY

Set for the weekend of June 12-15, the festival features a cross-section of artists amid multiple genres (if the headliners alone didn’t make that clear).

Produced and promoted by Superfly and AC Entertainment, Bonnaroo draws about 80,000 people a year and includes a comedy tent and film screenings.

There is also a mini-craft beer festival complete with beer classes, and a 5K race. Tickets go on sale February 22 at noon EST and start at $249.50.

As for another annual, acclaimed multi-day music event, the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival (often just called Coachella) is sold out already.

A reunited OutKast will anchor Coachella 2014, set for the weekends of April 11-13 and April 18-20 on a polo field in the desert city of Indio, Calif.

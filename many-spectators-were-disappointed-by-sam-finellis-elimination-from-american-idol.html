The much-anticipated Hollywood Week round was featured on American Idol Season 20 Episode 6, which aired on Monday. Contestants that received golden tickets advanced to the final round of auditions for a slot on the singing reality program. Sam Finelli was one of the competitors in the pop genre that was coached by Season 6 champion Jordin Sparks.

Finelli was shown in Episode 6 seeming a little anxious before stepping on stage. However, once he got up there, he delivered an outstanding performance. The live crowd applauded the artist as she sang Say Something by A Great Big World. Despite his strong performance, the judges did not advance him to the next round. Many viewers were disappointed by his removal.

What do fans have to say about it?

Finelli confessed before his performance that he is autistic, which makes it difficult for him to perform in front of an audience or crowd.

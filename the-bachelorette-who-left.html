Who won The Bachelorette 2023?

IT'S FAIR to say that The Bachelorette season 20 has been a rollercoaster of drama and romance for one woman trying to find her man.

Charity Lawson has now made her choice and picked one of the final two suitors in a tense grand finale.

Who won The Bachelorette 2023?

The Bachelorette concluded on August 21, 2023, with Charity having to choose between Dotun and Joey.

A tense finale then ensued, with viewers on the edge of their seat to see who would get picked.

In the end, Charity picked Dotun as her man, making him the 2023 winner.

At the site of the proposal, both were teary-eyed.

"You made me feel alive. You made me believe in love again. You made me feel so valued and so seen," Charity said. "I see you as my husband. I see you as my future. I see it so clear and I don't have any doubts. I love you so much."

Dotun the echoed this by saying: "You showed me that a good thing really can be just a good thing. The love that we have is perfect."

Who was in The Bachelorette 2023 final?

The semi-final saw Charity move forward with Dotun, Joey, and Xavier in Pacific Harbor, Fiji, as she gushed: "It is so romantic!"

However, Xavier was given the boot after he revealed that he cheated on his ex-girlfriend.

"I wanted to tell you that because I know that that is, like, very triggering for you," Xavier said, in reference to Charity's last relationship, which ended because of infidelity.

This meant that Dotun and Joey were the two finalists.

THE Kardashian family has seen some serious diamond bling through the years, from Kourtney Kardashian's engagement sparkler to Kim's subtle diamond band.

Men have come and gone from the family, but fans still remember the diamonds they gifted while they were around.

Kendall Jenner is the latest member of the famous family to don some gossip-worthy jewels.

The model got the rumor mill going after she shared a photo of herself and boyfriend Devin Booker sporting what looked like wedding jewelry.

In the photo, the NBA player appeared to be rocking a gold band on his ring finger.

Kendall and Devin are notoriously private.

They kept their romance under wraps for 10 months, going public on Valentine's Day in 2021.

Neither has commented on rumors they secretly tied the knot.

KIM KARDASHIAN

Kim Kardashian has been known to sport some flashy jewels on her fingers and elsewhere.

She's been married more than once, and has had some majorly impressive marital jewelry.

While her marriage to ex Kris Humphries didn't stand the test of time, he did a great job with her wedding jewelry.

He popped the question with a massive 19.8-carat engagement ring which featured a 16.2-carat center stone, according to the Los Angeles Times, and was designed to be a replica of a ring Kim wanted as a child.

After their marriage ended, Kris auctioned the ring off and got an impressive $749,000 for it.

She later got engaged to Kanye West, who proposed in a grand show of love at the stadium the Dodgers play at.

Per E! News, Kanye designed Kim's ring with the help of Lorraine Schwartz.

"The ring is flawless. Not just internally flawless, it's flawless. It's a D-stone, the best diamond there is, type 2A," Lorraine's rep told the outlet.

"It's a perfect cushion-cut diamond."

The ring was estimated to be worth about $3 million - $1 million more than Kris' was estimated to be worth.

It featured a massive center stone sat on a band with smaller diamonds surrounding it, totaling 15 to 20-carats in diamonds.

After a scary incident in Paris, Kim began sporting a more minimal style ring.

PEOPLE reported when she debuted the ring that it was a thin band covered in diamonds.

One of the first times she wore it, she also stepped out with a gold pinky ring on.

KHLOE KARDASHIAN

Khloe is no stranger to finger bling.

In 2009, she stepped out sporting a massive engagement ring from then-beau Lamar Odom.

The couple met and got married within weeks of knowing each other, but the NBA star cut no corners when it came to picking out Khloe's engagement and wedding rings.

Lamar popped the question with a radiant-cut ring in a halo setting, according to Business Insider.

The center diamond featured a stunning 12.5-carats, and the ring was estimated to be worth about $850,000.

Khloe also sported a wedding band covered in diamonds.

The couple officially divorced in 2016, and Khloe moved on.

Among the men she was linked to following her marriage to Lamar was rapper French Montana.

In 2014 - two years before her divorce was finalized - French surprised his then-girlfriend with three pieces of jewelry and a new Jeep, E! News reported at the time.

Among the jewels French gifted Khloe was a ring designed by Rafaello & Company.

CEO Gabriel Jacobs said at the time: "He mentioned how much Khloe loves flexible finger rings and was drawn to the Rafaello Couture collection."

The ring was made of 18k gold and featured a natural green Colombian emerald and yellow canary diamonds.

Years later, Khloe linked up with Tristan Thompson, whom she shares daughter True Thompson with.

The pair have had their ups and downs, but while they were doing well, the NBA player gifted the former Keeping Up With the Kardashians star with a massive ring.

Khloe has been spotted wearing the ring on Instagram on several occasions, sparking rumors that she and Tristan are engaged.

She shared a snap of herself sporting it on her wedding ring finger in February 2021, though she's been seen wearing it since as far back as July 2020.

Diamond expert Steve Stone told Hello Magazine the ring appears to be worth $2.5 million - $1.65 million more than the ring Lamar gave Khloe.

It features a pear-shaped center stone estimated to be about 13-carats and two smaller one-carat diamonds on either side.

Khloe was seen wearing other diamond rings on her wedding ring finger while dating Tristan too.

Before her massive diamond, she was spotted wearing three enormous diamond bands on the same finger.

As reported by Us Weekly at the time, she shared a photo of the rings on Snapchat, sparking early speculation about her relationship with Tristan.

At the time, she also had a large diamond ring on her right hand.

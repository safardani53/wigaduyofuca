JAZZ'S JOURNEY

Fans first met Jazz back in 2007 when she took part in a famous interview with Barbara Walters.

Then, in 2015 her show I Am Jazz first aired.

At the time, she was just a teenager and was documenting her struggles after transitioning from male to female at a young age.

The show has now aired more than seven seasons and Jazz has changed in a whole host of ways.

She has gained confidence in certain areas while continuing to struggle in others.

WEIGHT STRUGGLES

In November 2021, Jazz opened up about her weight gain, revealing that she put on the pounds after binging on doughnuts, bagels, and fast food.

She confessed in the Season 7 trailer that she was on a mission to lose weight and get into shape but confessed that she was struggling.

She also opened up on social media, sharing an honest note to her fans, reading: "As many of you have noticed, over the past few years, I have gained a substantial amount of weight.

"I suffer from binge-eating disorder, a disease in which I’m not only addicted to food, but I eat it in large quantities.

"My binging, along with an increased appetite I experience from some of the meds I’m on, has caused me to gain almost 100 pounds in a little less than 2 years."

The young web personality acknowledged her great support system and is set on making "positive changes" to her body.

She wrote: "I’m ready to take the initiative and create positive changes when it comes to my health and body.

"I have a fabulous team supporting me, both professionals and family/friends, but at the end of the day, I have to be the one committed toward bettering myself.

"I know I have the power in me to lose the weight, and I intend on sharing my progress with all of you. Love you all, and thanks for understanding the battle I’m fighting to win."

Included in the photo were Griffen's brother Sander, his sister Jazz, and their father Gregory Jennings.

The I Am Jazz stars posed side-by-side on what appeared to be a pickleball court.

They all had smiles on their faces and wore athletic clothing.

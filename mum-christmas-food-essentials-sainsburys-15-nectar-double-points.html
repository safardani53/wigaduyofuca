IT can be expensive getting enough food for Christmas - especially when boxes of the viral chocolates cost £5 a pop.

But one woman has shown how she got a huge feast of festive food and snacks for just £15 from Sainsbury's.

Lisa took to TikTok to share a video explaining how she'd managed to save herself loads on her Christmas haul.

"This just shows that shopping at Sainsbury's all year is a benefit," she said.

"I got all of this shopping today for £15.

"There was also a £6 Barbie book with this and a box of wine gums that my mum took out of the bag."

Lisa said that she'd collected £50 in Nectar points.

And thanks to the current scheme in Sainsbury's, she was able to double that to have £100 in points.

"And obviously Sainsbury's price matching Aldi a lot of the minute, so it's not that expensive anymore," Lisa added.

The total of her shopping was £115 - meaning she only had to shell out £15 on top of the points.

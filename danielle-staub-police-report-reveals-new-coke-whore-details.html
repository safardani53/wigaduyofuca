Danielle Staub Police Report Reveals New Coke Whore Details

June 21, 2024
blog

If The Real Housewives of New Jersey’s table-flipping finale and the show’s subsequent reunion special didn’t satisfy your Danielle Staub fix, this ought to.

The Smoking Gun has unearthed a 26-page manifesto on Staub’s 1986 arrest, which was mentioned in Kevin Maher’s 1995 memoir, Cop Without A Badge.

Also known as “THE BOOK.”

Some of the highlights shedding new light on Staub’s coke-whore past:

  • When Danielle Staub was living in Miami in 1986, she worked as an escort and went by Angela Minelli. One of Danielle’s johns was Daniel Claudio Aguilar.
  • Daniel, of course, was a major cocaine dealer for the Colombian drug cartels. In June of that year, Daniel sold two kilos of coke for $48,000 to some guys.
  • Danielle’s neighbor, Carmen Centolella, brokered the deal. Before the deal was final, Daniel sent Danielle to Carmen’s pad with a kilo of coke for “testing.”
  • The dudes who were going to buy the coke turned on Danielle, stole the drugs and took off. Daniel blamed Carmen, beat his ass and held him for ransom.
  • Danielle Staub was in on the kidnapping and made several calls to Carmen’s father demanding $25,000 for his son’s life. Carmen’s dad called the FBI.
  • They traced calls back to Daniel’s apartment and they found Danielle. 
  • They arrested Danielle Staub and confiscated $16,000 cash and six kilos of coke.
  • They also busted Daniel. To save herself, Danielle turned on Daniel and agreed to work with prosecutors. She pleaded guilty to one count of extortion.
  • When Danielle Staub got out of jail, she received several threatening phone calls. One from a lady who said, “Your life is at an end, honey.” Danielle and the authorities both believed the threats were coming from Daniel’s people.
  • Danielle’s apartment was also broken into several times. During Daniel’s trial, his lawyers pained Danielle as a prostitution whore. So to speak.
  • Daniel was found guilty and sent to prison. He got out in 1994.
  • When Danielle came off of probation in 1988, a doctor asked the court to keep her in a rehab program due to her drug history and lifestyle.

Amber Stevens Marries Former Greek Costar Andrew J. West: Photos

September 01, 2024
blog

It happened Greek fans! Former costars Amber Stevens and Andrew J. West tied the knot in L.A. on Friday, Dec. 5, his rep confirms to Us Weekly. Stevens wore a CB Couture dress for her special day as West donned a J.Crew tux.

The newlyweds and their guests noshed on a cake by Cake and Art in West Hollywood and their flower were provided by Fleur de Rye. Leading up to the big day, Stevens posted a throwback Instagram picture of her parents, radio announcer Shadoe Stevens and former model Beverly Cunningham, on their wedding day. “My inspiration #momanddad #weddingday #tbt,” she captioned the pic on Dec. 4.

She also shared that she went on the Bikini Cleanse in order to slip into her bridal dress with ease. “A bride’s breakfast #bikinicleanse #bikinibride #itsthefinalcountdown,” she captioned a shake pic.

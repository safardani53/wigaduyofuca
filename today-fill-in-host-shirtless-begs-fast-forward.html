A TODAY show fill-in host has gone shirtless in a new video and begged producers to skip ahead live on air.

Jacob Soboroff - who has been covering for several of Today's regular co-hosts throughout the week - became shy when sharing a clip from his weekend.

During the show's third hour on Monday, Jacob, 40, shared with his colleagues, Al Roker, 69, Craig Melvin, 44, and Jill Martin, 47, that he ran a half-marathon.

The group applauded their co-host for his major achievement while a video played of Jacob, donning shorts and no shirt, waving at the camera as he approached the finish line.

"No, it's embarrassing. And it's my first and last time shirtless on national television," the TV personality commented before yelling, "Fast forward it! Fast forward it!"

A photo then appeared on the screen of Jacob, wearing a black tank top, glasses, and a blue Adidas cap, posing with his college roommate, who joined him in the race.

Everyone laughed at Jacob's remark, and Craig assured his co-host: "You look great!"

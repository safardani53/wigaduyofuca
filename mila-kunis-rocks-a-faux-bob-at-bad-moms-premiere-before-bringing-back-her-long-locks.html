Mila Kunis Rocks a Shorter Hairdo at Bad Moms Premiere

July 08, 2024
blog

Kristina Bumphrey/StarPix/REX/Shutterstock

Mila Kunis surprised everyone with a noticeably shorter hairdo at the Bad Moms premiere in New York City on Monday. The pregnant actress not only looked stunning in a dark navy Ulla Johnson jumpsuit, but she also rocked a faux bob on the red carpet.

“Mila loved the short bob — she was completely up for it,” says the guy responsible for the look, Renato Campora. “Because of the jumpsuit, we wanted to show the neck line, creating a completely effortless hairstyle.”

To create Kunis’ faux bob, Campora first curled all of her stands with a 1.25” curling iron before weaving two small braids behind her ears and pinning them together, creating a cushion to hold the bob in place. Then, he simply tucked the her ends under and pinned large sections to the braids, giving a short-haired effect.

Robert Kamau/GC Images

The next day, however, Kunis went back to her old ways, flaunting gorgeous, long waves. But that’s not to say she wouldn’t experiment with a cropped look again. Campora says, “I’m sure she will try it again she felt modern, trendy and beautiful.”

Just a couple weeks ago, the mother of one was spotted leaving a Los Angeles salon with her signature long flowing mane. In addition, the star attended a special Bad Moms screening earlier this month sporting medium-length hair.

Kunis and husband Ashton Kutcher confirmed to PEOPLE exclusively on June 15 about their second pregnancy. The new addition will join the former That ’70s Show costars’ daughter Wyatt Isabelle, 20 months.

Bad Moms hits theaters July 29.

– Karen Mizoguchi

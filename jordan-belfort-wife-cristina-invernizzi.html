Who is Jordan Belfort's wife Cristina Invernizzi?

Cristina is a model and actress.

Her precise age is not known, but she is understood to be in her 30s and from Argentina.

She met her future husband, 59, in 2019 before marrying him in Last Vegas in February 2021, according to PageSix.

Cristina boasts 36,000 followers on Instagram where she regularly shares her modelling snaps.

She also frequently posts pictures of herself and Belfort in exotic locations around the world.

On International Women's Day, Belfort honoured his "truly beautiful" partner, saying: "You inspire me to be the best version of myself every day."

Who are Jordan Belfort's ex-wives?

Denise Lombardo

Belfort married his first wife Denise Lombardo in 1985.

Born in Ohio, Denise was raised in an Italian family and met her future husband at high school in Queens, New York.

They quickly went from childhood sweethearts to a married couple, though later divorced when Belfort's infidelity became public.

During his marriage to Denise, Belfort filed for bankruptcy as he dealt with repeated financial struggles.

He also began enjoying a life filled with drugs and extravagant parties.

Former stockbroker Belfort and Denise separated in 1991.

Nadine Caridi

Belfort married Nadine Caridi, now known as Nadine Macaluso, the same year he split from his first wife.

They were reportedly introduced by Caridi's ex-boyfriend Alan Wilzig.

Wilzig wrote on Facebook when The Wolf of Wall Street film came out: "Twenty-odd years ago while ‘The Wolf’ was still growing his fangs — I had my first post-college relationship.

"At the end of year two, as friendly exes I took Nadine to Jordan Belfort’s July 4th Westhampton Beach House party where the two of them me.

"He left his high school sweetheart wife to marry her; and the rest you can see in the movie."

Belfort and model-turned-actress Caridi had two children - Chandler, born in 1993, and Carter, born in 1996.

The couple later divorced in 2005.

Caridi was played by Margot Robbie in the 2013 film, though the actress told how it was not meant to be a true-to-life portrayal.

There is little information about what divorce settlement Caridi received, but Belfort hinted it might have been a hefty sum in an interview with Notable Life.

He said: "Sign a prenup, Don’t get yourself in a situation where if you get divorced you will screw yourself."

Caridi is now married to John Macaluso.

Anne Koppe

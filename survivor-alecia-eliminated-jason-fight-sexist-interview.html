Alecia Eliminated Fires Back At Horrible Role Model Jason Hollywood Life

Can you break down that insane idle chase?
Yes! You missed out a lot of that! I made a mistake by sharing the clue with Cyndey. I just wasn’t thinking. Anyway, me and Jason and he ended up finding it. What you didn’t see was Scott and Cydney were talking, so I got a machete and tried to chop down the tree; producers started yelling at me. So, I tried to climb up — I got half way but it was too windy. You also didn’t see that during that run to get it, Jason was standing next to me and with all his force, elbowed me down onto the rocks. He has absolutely no respect for women and is a bad role model for his daughters.

Do you feel like you were fairly portrayed?
I was ok out there! I did fight back a lot more than what was shown. They were just stereotyping me right away because I wasn’t as big, as them.

What’s your relationship with them now? What will it be like when you see them at the finale?
I don’t plan on having any type of conversation with them. I was more or less laughing at them. If you’re going to set that type of role model and have daughters, that’s all you.

When you were sent home, Scott said they hoped you’d get why they treated you that way when you watched back. Did you?
I find it comical. Scott telling me I’m a cheerleader? Really, most of your NBA career was spent on the bench cheering your team on, so you know all about cheerleading. He was literally the dinosaur of the tribe. He’s old, he’s lazy, he wasn’t even that beneficial in our challenges. I made the fire, and he quit on me and went to sleep. I sat out there in the sun for five hours. ‘Weak’ is not having food and water; if you’re just gonna quit and sleep all day, that’s weak. I don’t think I was a weak player.

What about Cyndney? She didn’t really stand up for you. Before that last tribal did you try to change her mind?
I thought I could trust her after the first episode, but I quickly learned I really couldn’t trust anyone in my tribe. I tried to get Cyndney to go against them and I tied to find the immunity idol for hours.

Would you do another season?
I’m dying to go back.

Was there anything you wish was shown that wasn’t?
At the first challenge, Cydney was trying to grab anything and everything, and she stuck fruit down her pants; when she got back it was spoiled so she threw it in the sand. I went and cleaned it and fed it to them. Another thing was Jason, who is all talk — he thinks he’s such a bad boy. Well, he let our chicken out and he couldn’t catch it, Darnell had to.

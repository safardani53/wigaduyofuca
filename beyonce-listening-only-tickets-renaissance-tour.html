Beyonce charging 122 for 'listening only' tickets for Renaissance Tour with no view of stage

July 12, 2024
blog

BEYONCÉ is selling £122 “listening only” tickets for her concerts — in seats that offer NO VIEW of the show.

The tickets for her Renaissance world tour, now on its US leg, are situated behind the stage and give no glimpse of the Crazy In Love singer.

One furious fan said: “The whole point of going to the concert is seeing her in person and watching the spectacle of the production.

“It’s ridiculous.

"If I only wanted to hear the concert, I’d stand outside in the car park.”

Tickets with a limited view, sometimes used by the blind or visually impaired, are normally a fraction of the price.

read more on beyonce

The 41-year-old singer started her 56-date tour in Stockholm in May.

She played to more than 240,000 fans during a five-night residency at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium in North London.

Paul McCartney, Ariana Grande, Dua Lipa and Lizzo are among celebrities who have attended her shows so far.

The tour will continue across North America until October.

Robin Lopez | Early Life

Robin Lopez | Career

Before his professional career, Robin Lopez played for his high school team, where he averaged 10.4 points, 8.8 rebounds, and 5.7 blocks. Back then, he also bagged the 2006 McDonald’s All-American.

He ended his Stanford journey just two years after he announced to enter the NBA in 2008.

Phoenix Suns

Robin Lopez entered the NBA league in 2008 when the Phoenix Suns drafted him as the 15th overall pick.

New Orleans Hornets

After his tenure with the Suns, Robin enrolled with the New Orleans Hornets through a three-team sign-and-trade deal. During a year with the team between 2012 and 2013, he made significant progress. 

He began to start almost every game of the season and also bagged multiple career-highs on the field. 

Portland Trail Blazers

The Hornets stood him up for the three-way trade alongside the Portland Trail Blazers and Sacramento Kings. As the Trail Blazers took him on the team, he spent two years with them.

Chicago Bulls

Milwaukee Bucks

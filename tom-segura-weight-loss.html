Tom Seguras Weight Loss Journey How He Lost Over 50 Lbs

April 08, 2024
blog

The well-known stand-up comedian Tom Segura embarked on a remarkable weight loss journey that saw him shed over 50 pounds.

Despite previously accepting his physical health as “okay,” Segura realized that his sedentary lifestyle and overeating habits were taking a toll on his overall well-being.

Determined to make a change, he decided to make changes and enlisted the help of a trainer, who became his companion on tour and provided guidance in fitness.

Through consistent workouts, adapting to available gym facilities, and embracing healthier eating habits, Segura gradually transformed his physique.

Tom Segura’s weight loss journey

Segura, a renowned comedian, embarked on a transformative weight loss journey that significantly impacted his life. 

Despite the ironic title of his 2014 Netflix special, Completely Normal, Segura candidly revealed that his life was in turmoil during that period. 

One year before the special’s release, he visited a doctor and received concerning health news. Tests indicated that he was overweight and had elevated muscle enzymes, specifically creatine kinase (CK), which can indicate muscle breakdown or stress. 

Tom Segura. (Source: seguratom/Instagram)

Reflecting on his past, Segura admitted that he weighed 255 pounds, contrary to the 245 pounds he mentioned in the special. 

The 44-year-old comedian said, “I was 255 pounds. In the special, I say I was 245 pounds, but I’m lying,” via M&F.

He had allowed bad lifestyle habits to take over, trying to convince himself that his physical health was acceptable when, deep down, he knew it wasn’t.

At the age of 34, Segura found himself completely sedentary and facing various health issues. Thankfully, he decided to make a change. 

The comedian started incorporating workouts into his routine, and audiences could visibly see a transformation in his appearance when comparing his 2016 special, Mostly Stories. 

However, it was during his 2018 special, Disgraceful, that the audience truly took notice of his remarkable weight loss. On stage, Segura proudly announced that he had shed 50 pounds.

Tom Segura won’t tour without his trainer these days

In the 2023 interview with GQ, Segura revealed that he asked the help of his trusted trainer and professional photographer, Shaun Nix, to accompany him on the road. 

Not only does Nix provide fitness guidance, but he also captures moments from Segura’s tour as his official photographer.

Segura and Nix share similar characteristics, including age and build, which further enhance their working relationship. 

He mentioned their dietary preferences, such as yogurt with mixed berries, omelets with tomatoes and mushrooms, and occasionally incorporating protein shakes or meal bars into their routines. 

Interestingly, Segura realized a significant change in his eating habits during this tour. Despite not consciously aiming for a specific weight, he noticed that he weighed the lowest he had in nearly two decades. 

He observed that his portions naturally decreased, and he no longer experienced constant hunger. This revelation made him realize how much he had been overeating in the past. 

Tom Segura’s workout plans while touring

Tom Segura maintains a strict workout routine while on the road, as he revealed in an interview with GQ. He and his trainer, Nix, prioritize daily workouts during their tour schedule. 

The workouts vary depending on the available facilities at the venues they visit. Segura mentioned that they lift weights around four or five times a week. Moreover, he said they don’t lift; they try to do heavier cardio.

They focus on compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and inclines when they have access to a well-equipped gym with barbells.
